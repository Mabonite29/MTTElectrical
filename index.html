About us

MTT Electrical Contractor and General Services

is a registered company dedicated to providing comprehensive electrical services, ranging from service provider applications to household installations, operating both locally and nationwide. Committed to staying at the forefront of electrical construction, we embrace modern technologies and methods to ensure optimal results. Led by a team of highly skilled technical personnel, many of whom have garnered experience from prominent construction firms in Laguna and the wider region, we prioritize client satisfaction by consistently meeting and exceeding their demands and expectations.

Values Statement

Mission

To understand our customers’ needs and work closely with them to provide and achieve quality service demands and expectations.



Vision

To be recognized by our valued customers and partners as the preferred source of electrotechnical solution and trusted builder of quality structure through innovative technology.

Values

Create a work environment based on sincerity, perseverance, integrity, “malasakit” team effort and open-mindedness.



History

MTT ELECTRICAL CONTRACTOR is registered in Calamba City Laguna since August 13, 2007. It is rendering services in the field of electrical service installations for clients of MERALCO from household service application assistance to Certificate of Electrical Inspection (CEI) application up to energization of service entrance.September 2010- 2011 MTT ELECTRICAL CONTRACTOR was engaged in marketing PLDT new service application particularly thru surveying possible different areas in Calamba. After 2 years, MTT ELECTRICAL CONTRACTOR ventured into civil works to cater to clients that require concrete service post before electrical service installation. Individual clients and even whole subdivisions acquired these services.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, give us a quick phone call. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the site, what sort of interactivity the site will have, your graphic design needs, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. If you’re still interested, we’ll come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long it takes to design?

The time limit of any assignment is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you. The main general delay in the making of a website is waiting for the content of the pages to be sent to us by the client.

+

How do I associate with MTT Company?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of development.

+

Can you help my current site look more professional?

Yes. Give us your requirements and we have experienced expertise to help you give a new professional look that really wonders!

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal, and all major credit cards.
